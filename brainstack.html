Digital Service Innovation Project

Within organizations, knowledge is distributed over many different platforms and is often hard to find. This problem is especially apparent for new employees that onboard and struggle to find important information they need to start contributing.

Brainstack is a digital service my team and I created that centralizes and organizes knowledge for efficient employee onboarding from any source like Confluence, Medium, Slack, Outlook, JIRA, newsletters, etc. ​ This webpage walks you through our innovation process and how our final solution was created.

Our initial plan was to market this product to all working professional instead of making it specific for the onboarding process. To develop our product, we wanted to hypothesize about the customers we wanted to meet the needs of. Our goal was to develop a narrative to clearly understand the current state of the product. ​ First, we collectively developed an Analysis-Synthesis Bridge model, competitor analysis, PESTLE analysis, POG and Value-Flow model to organize our thoughts.
