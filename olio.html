Prioritize Your Digital Life

Olio is a start-up company that my team and I created for the McGinnis Venture Competition, which we are currently in the final round for. I created a concept video for our product, and my team and I worked on a Business Plan (click below to view) that we will be presenting to the judges. Given my experience with user centered research and evaluation and design, I am the primary Design and User Interface lead for Olio.

Olio is a smart web browser extension for personal management. It allows users to create customizable dashboards on their computers that integrate various websites, desktop apps, and productivity tools to create simple, easily accessible, adaptive, and visually stunning displays. Olio encourages prioritization and saves time by providing smart suggestions on what features best suit each user’s work habits.

Simply put, Olio brings together the user's day-to-day life onto a single page that pops up each time you open up a new tab.

Problem

Our first step involved identifying the problem area

On average, 49% of workers say they have difficulty locating documents. With the sudden transition to online life, users feel that all their information is scattered. Current organizational solutions don’t focus on prioritization, but try to consolidate as many services as possible onto a single page.

Additionally, these solutions come with a high initial commitment as they often do not integrate seamlessly with other tools that people use.

Solution

Once we identified the problem and conducted research about it, we created a wireframe with features we thought would be necessary to address it.

Olio is a smart web browser extension for personal management. Olio lets users create easily accessible, adaptive, and visually stunning dashboards through app integrations in the form of widgets. These widgets allow users to get started quickly without worrying about the worry of importing information from another app. Users can create and edit their dashboards by simply dragging and dropping the widgets. This dashboard can be accessed by opening a new tab on the browser. Olio encourages prioritization and saves time by providing smart suggestions on what features best suit each user’s work habits.

Olio brings together the user's day-to-day life onto a single page.
