Sticky Note Art

RED Themed Doodles

Overview

Every day, I spend 5-10 minutes creating a doodle on a post-it note and sticking it up on the wall my desk leans against. I use a red pen, a black pen, and a pencil to add my own twist to each drawing. I take inspiration from movies, TV shows, books, lecture notes, or anything interesting I come across and make doodles that match the theme RED, which is also the overall theme of my bedroom.

Mindset

Although I do this as a hobby, looking at my wall covered in sticky notes every day while working is how I inspire myself. Seeing the twists I add to characters/images/scenes and observing the simple and sometimes humorous drawings helps me destress-- I use this process as a way to reflect my thoughts and feelings. From this routine, I learned that artistic expression can improve one's outlook and mood, and doodling is my small way of communicating my thoughts, staying motivated, and being creative.
